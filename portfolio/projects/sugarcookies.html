Sugar Cookie Recipe!

This is a quick and easy recipe for sugar cookies. From start to finish it should not take you more than 20-30 minutes. You can add any kind of candy you want to the top before you bake them.

The number of cookies this recipe makes is dependent on how big or small you make your cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda.
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder.
  • 1 cup softened butter.
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar.
  • 1 egg.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • You can find everything you need Here.

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the dry ingredients.(flour, baking soda, and baking powder).
  3. Then in a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar untill smooth.
  4. Then beat in the egg and vanilla.
  5. next you slowly mix in the dry ingredients. Remember not to over mix your dough!
  6. Take about a teaspoon full of dough at a time and roll into a ball and place on cookie sheet.
  7. Let them bake for 8-10 minutes or untill golden brown.
  8. Remove from oven and place on a wire rack to cool.
